### The Problem: The Minimalist Routing

You have four nodes in your network—**Alpha, Beta, Gamma, and Delta**—that must be connected in a single continuous loop (a Hamiltonian cycle).

* **Constraint 1:** The path must be exactly 10 units long.
* **Constraint 2:** Each node must be visited exactly once before returning to the start.
* **Constraint 3:** You have the following edge weights:
* Alpha to Beta: 1
* Beta to Gamma: 4
* Gamma to Delta: 2
* Delta to Alpha: 3
* Alpha to Gamma: 6
* Beta to Delta: 5



**What is the specific sequence of nodes that completes this 10-unit circuit?**

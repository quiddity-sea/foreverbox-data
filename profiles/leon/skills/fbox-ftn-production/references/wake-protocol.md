# Wake Protocol

## Step-by-Step Initialization

1. Confirm today's date and identify the corresponding signal from the Seven Signals calendar
2. Calculate the 6-day sourcing window: target publish date minus six days
3. Read `/foreverbox_data/FromTheNoise_Active/leads_inbox.md` for existing leads
4. If inbox is empty or has no leads in the current window, run Tavily searches
5. Long-form days (Mon-Thu): generate 4-6 Story Lead Cards
6. Short-form days (Fri-Sun): generate 1-2 Story Lead Cards
7. Present all leads to the Human Director for selection
8. Proceed to Research Pack development for selected leads only
